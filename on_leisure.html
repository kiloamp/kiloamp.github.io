wood

wood is cool for diy projects, its cheap, there are many times of it, it looks cool in lighter or darker shades, it looks modern yet old, it has a great texture, its (more or less) natural and its cheap second-hand and super reusable

avionics

they are strangely cool to look at, time-critial systems that just do one function, like an oven or a microwave, only that they display the output of multibillion investments and thousands of kilometers of cabling in one (small but big enough) 100% functional display, sometimes i feel like many car designers should develop their systems based on avionics

thinkpads

idk man, they are just cool - so functional, so robust, great keyboards, fantastic battery life, really cheap second hand, high linux-value, very cool design
