LFS19 Configurator

Welcome to our car configurator. Let us take you through a quick tour

Before you start, we just wanted to thank you for your trust in the LUMotorsport team as the makers of your new single seat race car!

The Configurator has a navigation pane which will directly take you to different parts of the car, however if you just scroll down you will find all the parts in their respective order.

The configurator will offer to load the Stock Setup of the part, or None in the case you wish to personally work in that part by yourself, this will also reduce the price of the car overall. There is also the Custom option, which will allow us to work with you in order to find the perfect setup based on your needs, this option will add overall cost to the car and is only suggested for more experienced drivers that know what they need but don't have the means to produce it.

If you select the custom option we will ask you to drag and drop a .pdf of the technical drawing of the part you require - and the department experts will be in touch shortly to confirm the designs and manufacturing process.

Once the car is configured we will ask for your email and phone number, so that we can contact you and make sure you understand everything before making the purchase.

Enjoy configuring the car!

Sincerely
LUMotosport
